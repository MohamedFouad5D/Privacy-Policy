iHERITAGE-ICT Augmented Reality Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 30, 2023

Introduction:

Welcome to iHERITAGE-ICT, an augmented reality (AR) application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information and data when you use our AR app. By using iHERITAGE-ICT, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection:

We may collect the following types of information from you when you use iHERITAGE-ICT:

  • Device Information: We may collect device-related information, including the device model, operating system, and other technical data.
  • Camera and Sensor Data: iHERITAGE-ICT may access your device's camera and sensors to enable AR functionality.
  • Location Data: If the AR app requires location data to function properly, we may collect and process this information with your consent.
  • User Interactions: We may collect data related to your interactions and inputs within the AR app.

Use of Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing AR Functionality: We use the data to enable augmented reality features and deliver the intended functionality of iHERITAGE-ICT.
  • Improving Performance: The information helps us improve the performance and user experience of the app.
  • Personalization: We may personalize your experience within the app based on the collected data.
  • Analytics: We use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the AR app, allowing us to optimize and enhance its performance.
  • Marketing and Promotion: If you have provided consent, we may use your data for marketing and promotional purposes related to iHERITAGE-ICT.

Sharing of Information:

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • AR Service Providers: We may share data with third-party AR service providers to support and enhance the AR functionality.
  • Advertising Partners: If advertising is part of iHERITAGE-ICT, we may share data with advertising partners to deliver relevant ads.
  • Analytics Services: We may share data with analytics and data analytics services to analyze app usage and trends.

Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. We employ industry-standard security measures and encryption to safeguard your data.

Third-Party Links and Services:

iHERITAGE-ICT may include links to third-party websites or services. This privacy policy does not cover those external sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

Children's Privacy:

iHERITAGE-ICT is not intended for use by children under the age of 3 years. If you are under 3 years old, please do not use the app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 3 years old without parental consent. If you believe we have unintentionally collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to the Privacy Policy:

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this privacy policy, please contact us at ccbm.organisation@gmail.com.
